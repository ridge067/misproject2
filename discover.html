Discover UMD & Duluth

Why I chose University of Minnesota Duluth and what I enjoy about Duluth.

UMD Video (30s)

Why UMD

I chose the University of Minnesota Duluth for several compelling reasons that align with my academic and personal goals.

  • Strong Business Program: UMD's Labovitz School of Business and Economics offers excellent programs that will prepare me for a successful career in business and management.
  • Small Class Sizes: The smaller student-to-faculty ratio means more personalized attention and better learning opportunities.
  • Beautiful Campus: The campus is located in a stunning natural setting with Lake Superior nearby, providing an inspiring environment for learning.
  • Research Opportunities: UMD offers numerous undergraduate research opportunities that will enhance my academic experience.
  • Affordable Education: As a Minnesota resident, UMD provides excellent value for a quality education.

Duluth & Surroundings

Duluth offers an incredible quality of life that perfectly complements my academic pursuits and personal interests.

  • Outdoor Recreation: With Lake Superior, numerous hiking trails, and winter sports opportunities, Duluth is perfect for someone who loves outdoor activities like fishing and snowboarding.
  • Vibrant Community: The city has a thriving arts scene, great restaurants, and a welcoming community atmosphere.
  • Career Opportunities: Duluth's growing business community provides excellent internship and job opportunities for students.
  • Four Seasons: I love experiencing all four seasons, from beautiful summers on the lake to snowy winters perfect for snowboarding.
  • Proximity to Nature: The Boundary Waters Canoe Area and North Shore are easily accessible for weekend adventures.

My Goals at UMD

During my time at UMD, I plan to:

  • Excel academically in my business studies while maintaining a strong GPA
  • Get involved in student organizations and leadership opportunities
  • Participate in internships to gain real-world business experience
  • Take advantage of the outdoor recreation opportunities in the Duluth area
  • Build lasting relationships with professors and fellow students
  • Explore career paths in business and management
